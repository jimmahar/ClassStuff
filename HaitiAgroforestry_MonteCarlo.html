Model methodology: Carbon prices follow Geometric Brownian Motion (GBM) — the same framework used in Black-Scholes options pricing. Each trial generates a price path: P(t) = P(0) · exp[(μ − ½σ²)t + σ√t · Z] where μ is the drift rate, σ is volatility, and Z ~ N(0,1). Volatility widens each year by the growth rate you set, producing a realistic fan of uncertainty. Survival rates and hurricane events are drawn independently each trial. NPV is computed by discounting net annual cash flows at the chosen rate.
